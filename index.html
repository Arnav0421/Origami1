Interesting Facts About chameleon

  • Chameleons are part of the reptile family. As a reptile they are cold blooded. This means that what ever the temperature outside is their body temperature is that warm. That is why they generally live in more Tropical places like Florida.
  • Chameleons can change the color of their skin so that they can be "invisible" for preadators
  • Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches
